About

InfluencerStuff is poised to take on the management/agency model that current exists. We charge a simple low flat rate instead of hiking the prices up for sponsorships to make our percentages bigger. We want to focus on actually managing each aspect of your business and keep you doing what you do best, creating content! We offer in-depth analytics for each sponsorship done and work with some of the best brands out there.Transparency is our goal and we will continue to work towards making this industry a fun and honest place to work in. InfluencerStuff provides anything from in-house Merch to in-depth campaign development. We offer brands a friendly and experienced influencer platform.

Our job is to make sure you know about every opportunity out there. Our goal is to make sure each opportunity makes sense for the audience you have or are trying to reach.

We bridge the gap between influencers and brands, developing partnerships that last longer and engage more with each and every audience member. Each influencer and brand is different so treating each as such does no good for anyone! Go ahead and fill out our forms down below and lets start discussing how InfluencerStuff can help you!
